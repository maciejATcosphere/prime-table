Use the input component above to type you range patterns. The following patterns are acceptable:
  • 1/3/20 ≡ 1st, 3rd and 20th - 28th primes
  • 7-20 ≡ 7th - 20th primes
  • 18+ ≡ 18th - 37th primes
  • 1/3/14+ ≡ 1st, 3rd and 14th - 33th primes
  • 1/3-7/14 ≡ 1st, 3rd - 7th and 14th - 17th primes
  • 1/3-7/14+ ≡ 1st, 3rd - 7th and 14th - 27th primes
  • 1/3-7/9-11/14 ≡ 1st, 3rd - 7th and 9th - 11th and 14th primes
  • If you will go with something crazy like 70000+ prepare yourself for few seconds of waiting
Use Ctrl + Up, Ctrl + Left Ctrl + Down and Ctrl + Right to slide through consecutive primes and generate their multiplication table.
For example, to close your browser hit Cmd + Q
